Yeeching Lau

24-year-old Hong Kong Pianist Yeeching Lau is active as a soloist, accompanist, chamber musician and repetiteur. Yeeching performs both solo and chamber music extensively. She was invited by British Isle Music Festival as a soloist and chamber pianist in 2023, has worked on Royal Birmingham Conservatoire 2024 Spring Opera: Cinderella as repetiteur, and she is the winner of the Edward Brooks Lieder Accompanist Prize in 2024.
